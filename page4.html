Train Lines

LRT Line 1

The LRT Line 1 runs from Baclaran to Roosevelt. It is one of the oldest lines in Manila.

LRT Line 2

The LRT Line 2 runs from Recto to Santolan, connecting important areas in Manila.

MRT Line 3

The MRT Line 3 runs along EDSA, providing a fast route across the city.
