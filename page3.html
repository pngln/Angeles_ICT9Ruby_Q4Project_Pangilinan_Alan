Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  3. Combine and pour into a baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1 sponge cake
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. Slice strawberries and mix with sugar.
  2. Layer sponge cake, strawberries, and whipped cream.
  3. Chill before serving.
